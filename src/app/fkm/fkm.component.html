Free Kick Master

Free Kick Master is, as its name implies, a soccer Free Kick mini game for mobile phones. It features a cross-platform competitive leaderboard for an "arcade" experience. As of my mission statement, FKM doesn't feature any ads or in-app purchases. It was made in my first year of college using Unity, Blender, Gravit Designer, Visual Studio and of course a lot of tutorials, trials, errors and dedication.
