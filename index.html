Hi, I'm Malick a 20 year old gamer from Senegal.

Having played games my whole life, I always dreamed of one day making my own.
With no intentions of letting go of this goal, I traveled to France after my high-school graduation to take Software Engineering classes thus beginning my journey.

Now in my second year I have already released my first game and am working on the second one as you read this.

What makes my games special you may ask? Well, It's simple I may answer !
They're made by a gamer first and foremost.
This means no experience-deterring practices such as ads or microtransactions.
Why? Because it's all supposed to be fun and games !

Free Kick Master is, as its name implies, a soccer Free Kick mini game for mobile phones.
It features a cross-platform competitive leaderboard for an "arcade" experience.
As of my mission statement, FKM doesn't feature any ads or in-app purchases.
It was made in my first year of college using Unity, Blender, Gravit Designer, Visual Studio and of course a lot of tutorials, trials, errors and dedication.

Home Sweet Home is an upcoming 2d platformer set in Africa.
It's set for release in August 2021 on mobile phones.
As of my mission statement, HSH won't feature any ads or in-app purchases.
I'm currently making it in my second year of college using Unity, Sketchbook, Gravit Designer, Visual Studio and again a lot of tutorials, trials, errors and dedication.
